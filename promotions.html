IFWL Mid-Season Review Explained

At Race 5, IFWL reviews all accumulated points from each driver.

For drivers who were AM and are now moving to Pro, IFWL will deduct 15% of their accumulated points.

For drivers who are moved down from Pro to AM, IFWL will add 15% of their accumulated points.

IFWL has an add / deduct cap limit of 30 points maximum either way at Race 5.
