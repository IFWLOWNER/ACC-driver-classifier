BEFORE YOU SUBMIT YOUR TICKET

IFWL does not enforce a mandatory camera angle requirement, as our championships run across both console and PC and we appreciate that not every driver has the same capture options available.

That said, our stewarding team strongly encourages drivers to provide as much detail as possible in the incident summary, the video footage submitted, and any statements provided.

The image above shows the ideal replay/camera style for steward reviews, as it helps our team clearly see steering, throttle, brake and general driver input behaviour. Where possible, we would greatly appreciate both the reporting driver and the accused driver sharing footage from this type of view.

We fully understand this may not always be possible for every user — but in general, the more information our stewards have, the better equipped they are to review each incident fairly and accurately.
