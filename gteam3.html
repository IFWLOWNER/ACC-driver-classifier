Series Information

All ACC tracks • All DLC packs used

Mandatory Pit + Stint Rules

• Each race is 3 hours.

• All 3 drivers must race.

• Each driver must complete at least 45 minutes.

• Minimum 2 pit stops.

• Driver swaps must happen in pit lane.

If a driver doesn’t meet minimum time, the team may be disqualified.

Driver Order Rotation

• You cannot use the same starting driver two rounds in a row.

• Rotate starters: Driver A → Driver B → Driver C.

Team Points

• Points are awarded to the team.

Reserve Driver Handling

• Each team may register 1 reserve.

• Reserve may race a maximum of 2 rounds.

• Reserve must be similar pace to the replaced driver.

• Points always go to the team.
